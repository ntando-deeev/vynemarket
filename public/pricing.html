Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Free plan really free forever? +
Yes — 100% free, no credit card required, no expiry. You can list your business and reach customers worldwide at no cost.
How does the customer invite feature work? +
On Pro, you get a unique invite link and QR code for each listing. Share it on WhatsApp, Instagram, or print it on your packaging. Customers who click it join your community feed and get your broadcasts instantly.
What is a broadcast? +
A broadcast is a message you send to all your community followers at once — like a sale announcement, new product launch, or update. It shows up in their VyneMarket feed.
What does competitor analytics show? +
Growth Suite users can see how their listing performs vs. the category average — views, leads, ratings — and see the top 5 competitors in their category by traffic.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. You can downgrade to Free at any time. Your listing stays live, you just lose the paid features.
